Fiber-Composite-Lab Experiment Manual
空间连续纤维增强复合材料原位成型实验操作规程
======================================================

Overview
--------
This experiment performs in-situ forming of continuous carbon-fiber (CF)
reinforced PEEK composite samples aboard the space station. The procedure
must be executed in strict sequence to satisfy FSM constraints and obtain
BACC (Beijing Aerospace Flight Control Center) authorizations at critical
decision points.

Phase 1 — Environment Preparation
-----------------------------------
1. Enable the vacuum exhaust system (enable_vacuum_exhaust) as the very
   first step. The exhaust prevents conductive CF debris from floating and
   short-circuiting cabin electronics. All subsequent steps require the
   vacuum to be RUNNING.

2. Preheat the extrusion nozzle to 380°C (preheat_nozzle). Nozzle reaches
   READY state once temperature is within ±2°C of setpoint (~5 min).

3. Preheat the ceramic build platform to 150°C (preheat_platform).
   This improves first-layer PEEK adhesion in microgravity.

4. Execute the print-head homing sequence (home_printhead) to zero the
   motion system and confirm filament tension is in the 0.5–1.2 N range.

Phase 2 — Authorization and First Layer
-----------------------------------------
5. Request experiment start authorization from BACC
   (request_bacc_authorization). Wait for telemetry confirmation before
   proceeding. This is a mandatory ground-link checkpoint.

6. Begin first-layer carbon-fiber deposition (start_first_layer). Requires
   all four subsystems to be in their READY/RUNNING states and BACC
   authorization to be received.

7. Scan the spreading ratio of the first layer (scan_spreading_ratio).
   Target ratio is 1.2–1.8. Nominal plans assume acceptable ratio; adjustments
   are handled by the replanner if the ratio is out of range.

Phase 3 — Multilayer Forming
------------------------------
8. Ramp fiber tension from start tension to forming tension
   (ramp_fiber_tension) before proceeding to additional layers.

9. Execute the main forming sequence (execute_main_forming). This is a
   non-interruptible skill that requires human-in-the-loop approval before
   dispatch.

10. Cut the fiber at the end of the forming run (cut_fiber).

Phase 4 — Inspection and Closure
----------------------------------
11. Inspect the composite sample for voids or delamination
    (inspect_composite). Records quality metrics.

12. Disable the vacuum exhaust system (disable_vacuum_exhaust) once all
    debris risk has passed and the build is complete.

Safety constraints:
- Nozzle temperature must not exceed 400°C.
- Vacuum flow must remain ≥10 L/min while CF material is exposed.
- execute_main_forming requires explicit HITL approval.
