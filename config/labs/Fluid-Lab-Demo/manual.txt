Fluid-Lab-Demo Experiment Manual
=================================

Overview
--------
This experiment circulates fluid through a heated sample chamber while a
camera records visual data. The procedure has three phases: startup,
observation, and shutdown.

Phase 1 — Startup
-----------------
1. Activate the fluid circulation pump (activate_pump). The pump must be
   running before any heating begins.
2. Once the pump is confirmed active, heat the sample to 40°C (heat_to_40).
3. Start camera recording (activate_camera) to capture fluid dynamics
   during the heating phase.

Safety constraint: temperature must not exceed 45°C. If the thermal
subsystem reports a temperature above this threshold, issue an
emergency_stop immediately.

Phase 2 — Observation
---------------------
1. Optionally read telemetry (read_telemetry) to verify flow_rate ≥ 10 L/min
   and temperature is stable at 40 ± 1°C.
2. Maintain camera recording throughout the observation window.

Phase 3 — Shutdown
------------------
1. Cool the thermal subsystem back to ambient (cool_down) before stopping
   the pump.
2. Stop camera recording (deactivate_camera).
3. Deactivate the pump (deactivate_pump) after cool-down is confirmed.

In an anomaly or end-of-mission scenario, call emergency_stop which
performs the shutdown sequence automatically.
