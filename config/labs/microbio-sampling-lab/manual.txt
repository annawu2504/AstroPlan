Microbio-Sampling-Lab Experiment Manual
天和核心舱空气微生物采样实验操作规程
=================================================

Overview
--------
This experiment collects passive air microbial samples from the Tianhe core
module using agar petri dishes, then cultures the samples in an onboard
incubator and photographs colony growth for ground analysis. The procedure
requires strict sterile technique and ground authorization before opening
petri dishes.

Phase 1 — Pre-Experiment Setup
---------------------------------
1. Clear the cabin: direct non-experiment crew to leave the Tianhe core
   module and close the hatch (clear_cabin). The experimenter dons gloves,
   mask, and sterile garment.

2. Sterilize the incubator interior with isopropanol cloth (sterilize_incubator).

3. Pre-heat the incubator to 36 ±1°C (preheat_incubator). The incubator
   must reach temperature before petri dishes are opened.

4. Retrieve and inspect the three agar petri dishes from cargo package 9-2;
   wipe surfaces and attach position labels (prepare_petri_dishes).

Phase 2 — Pre-Sampling Checks
-------------------------------
5. Confirm the incubator has reached 36°C setpoint (verify_incubator_ready).
   Dishes must not be opened until the incubator is READY.

6. Fix the prepared petri dishes at anchor positions 2, 15, and 60 using
   Velcro straps (mount_petri_dishes). Confirm secure locking.

7. Report status to ground control and request sampling authorization
   (request_sampling_authorization). Do not open dishes until authorization
   is received.

Phase 3 — Air Sampling
------------------------
8. Open all three petri dish lids simultaneously (open_petri_dishes).
   The experimenter holds breath and retreats ≥2 m from each dish.
   The 20-minute passive sampling timer begins.

9. After exactly 20 minutes, seal all petri dishes with parafilm
   (seal_petri_dishes). Do not touch the agar surface.

Phase 4 — Incubation and Analysis
------------------------------------
10. Transfer the sealed petri dishes into the pre-warmed incubator
    (transfer_to_incubator). Incubation runs 48–72 hours at 36°C.

11. After incubation, photograph colony morphology with the high-resolution
    camera and upload images to the ground database (photograph_colonies).

12. Package archived petri dishes into the bio-sample recovery box for
    return to Earth (package_for_return).

Safety constraints:
- Dishes may only be opened after cabin is CLEARED and ground authorization
  is received.
- Incubator must be READY (36°C) before open_petri_dishes.
- seal_petri_dishes requires explicit HITL approval (non-interruptible).
